Welcome to Anointed Chapel, Brampton

A worship center for all nations where joy and hope are restored. Our goal is to prepare men and women to fulfill their purpose in life as well as be ready for the soon coming King.

We are committed to teaching the undiluted word of God so that we manifest the Total Man God has called us to be. We also believe strongly in reaching out to the community around us and demonstrating the love that Christ first showed us.

Timothy Oyeyinka

Pastor
